🌲 Privacy overview

Road Trip Camping Adventure is an immersive open-world exploration game where you drive through scenic landscapes, set up camp, gather resources, and survive the wilderness. We respect your privacy as much as we respect nature. This policy explains what information we collect, why, and how you stay in full control — because your privacy is as important as your adventure.

Our promise: No selling of personal data, minimal permissions, full compliance with COPPA/GDPR-K/CCPA. We're here to help you explore, not to track you.

📊 1. What data we collect

Road Trip Camping Adventure is designed to be lightweight and privacy-respecting. We only collect anonymous, non‑identifiable information to improve the game and show optional ads:

  • 📱 Device & technical info — model, OS version, language, country, advertising identifier (AAID / IDFA), IP address (anonymized). Used for analytics & ads.
  • 🎮 Game progress & preferences — campsite upgrades, unlocked areas, inventory items, sound settings. Stored locally on your device.
  • 📢 Ad interaction data — via Google AdMob. AdMob may use identifiers to serve contextual or non-personalized ads. No personal data is shared.
  • 💎 In-app purchases — if you buy premium camping gear or remove ads, Google Play Billing processes the transaction. We never see your payment details.

We never access your contacts, media gallery, location, or any personal files. Your camping spot is your own.

⚙️ 2. How we use your data

Your information helps us deliver the most immersive and smooth camping adventure:

  • Save game progress — remember your campsite, inventory, and exploration milestones.
  • Analyze & improve performance — using Firebase Analytics & Crashlytics to fix bugs and enhance gameplay.
  • Show non-intrusive ads (optional banners and rewarded videos) to keep the game free.
  • Verify in-app purchases and grant virtual items.
We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their own marketing.

🔌 3. Third‑party services (SDKs)

To deliver core features, we integrate with trusted partners:

  • Google AdMob – advertising. May collect device data and IP for ad relevance. Google Privacy Policy.
  • Firebase Analytics & Crashlytics – anonymized crash reports and usage metrics. Helps us keep Road Trip Camping Adventure stable and fun.
  • Google Play Billing – secure handling of in-app purchases. We never see payment details.

All third parties comply with GDPR, CCPA, COPPA, and other applicable privacy frameworks.

🗑️ 4. Data retention & deletion

Your campsite and progress remain on your device. Uninstalling Road Trip Camping Adventure removes all local data. No server-side account exists. To request deletion of any analytics data linked to your advertising ID, email us at nadeemgamer180@gmail.com. We'll respond within 30 days.

You can reset your advertising ID anytime: Android Settings → Google → Ads → Reset advertising ID.

🎯 5. Your ad choices

We respect your advertising preferences:

  • On Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization.
  • You may also purchase the “Remove Ads” feature (one‑time IAP) to enjoy an ad‑free camping experience.
  • For users under the age of consent, only non‑personalized contextual ads are shown.
Child‑safe compliance (COPPA / GDPR‑K) enabled

🧸 6. Children's privacy (COPPA / GDPR‑K)

Road Trip Camping Adventure is designed for all ages and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 (or under 16 in the EEA).

  • No personal data (name, email, contact) is required to play the game.
  • If the Play Store identifies a child account, personalized ads are automatically disabled.
  • Parents: if you believe your child has provided any personal information (e.g., via customer support), contact us immediately and we will delete it.

🔒 7. Security measures

We implement industry‑standard security: encrypted network connections (TLS), minimal data retention, and restricted internal access. While no system is 100% invulnerable, we continuously update our practices to protect your information.

📜 8. Your privacy rights

Depending on your location (EEA, UK, California, Brazil, India), you may have the right to:

  • Access any personal data we might hold (linked to advertising ID).
  • Request correction or deletion of such data.
  • Opt out of “sale” of personal data (we never sell).
  • Withdraw consent for analytics by resetting your advertising ID.

To exercise any right, email nadeemgamer180@gmail.com with subject “Privacy Request – Road Trip Camping Adventure”. We will respond promptly.

🔄 9. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. If changes are significant, we will notify you via an in‑app notice or update the effective date above. Continued use of Road Trip Camping Adventure after changes means you accept the revised policy.
